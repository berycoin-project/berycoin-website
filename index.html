Berycoin has a fixed Block reward of 200 BERY each Block - No halving or decrement in block reward

Scrypt algorithm

Frequently Ask Questions

Here are some most frequently asked questions.

What is Berycoin?

Berycoin is a cryptocurrency based upon scrypt algorithm, It has 1 minute blocks with reward of 200 coins, Block reward is fixed e.g it will never decrease or halve until all the supply is mined

How much is Max coin supply?

Berycoin has a supply of 50 Billion coins, among them 20% of coin are premined remaining 80% coins can be mined over the period of 100+ years.

Premined coins. What for?

Premined coins are dedicated to project Berymedia, through which it will be distributed among people.

What is berymedia?

Berymedia is a platform, which promotes open culture, everyone is appreciated to join the platform, write articles/Post Projects/start courses and earn reward which will payed from premined coin supply.

Does this project available worldwide?

Yes. anyone from anywhere around the world can join us.
