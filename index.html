What is Berycoin?

Berycoin is a peer-to-peer virtual Internet currency that enables instant payments globally, berycoin features 1 minute blocks with fixed block reward of 200 BERY. Berycoin itself is a decentral payment system, it works without having a need for some central authority. It secures transactions using cryptographic hashing and enables people to manage their finances by themselves. Berycoin comes with a purpose of promoting open culture projects, articles and courses.

Frequently Ask Questions

Here are some most frequently asked questions.

What is Berycoin?

Berycoin is a cryptocurrency based upon scrypt algorithm, It has 1 minute blocks with fixed block reward of 200 berycoins, e.g it will never decrease or halve until all the supply is mined

How much is Max coin supply?

Berycoin has a supply of 50 Billion coins, 20% of the supply is premined while remaining 80% coins can be mined over the period of 100+ years.

Premined coins. What for?

Premined coins are dedicated to project Berymedia, through it all of them, will be distributed among people.

What is berymedia?

Berymedia is a kind of social platform, which promotes open culture. You can post projects, work on existing projects, create courses or write articles and earn a reward for it.

Is this project available worldwide?

Yes. Anyone from anywhere around the world can join us.
